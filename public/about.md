## Our Process

### Discovery (Week 1)
We begin by understanding your business, industry, competitors, goals, and target audience. This phase includes a comprehensive questionnaire, market research, and a strategy session to align on objectives.

### Design (Weeks 2-3)
We create bespoke visual identity with wireframes, low-fidelity prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups in Figma. You see every screen before a single line of code is written. We iterate based on your feedback until every detail is perfect.

### Development (Weeks 4-7)
We build your website using Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. Every component is engineered for performance, accessibility, and responsiveness. We integrate CMS, analytics, forms, and any third-party services your project requires.

### Testing (Week 8)
Rigorous quality assurance across devices (mobile, tablet, desktop), browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge), and screen sizes. We benchmark performance, validate accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA), and test all forms and integrations.

### Launch
Zero-downtime deployment to Vercel with Cloudflare CDN. SSL configuration, DNS setup, Google Search Console configuration, analytics installation, and SEO finalization. Your website goes live without disruption.

### Support (1 Year Included)
Priority support including bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, content changes, and technical assistance. We monitor uptime, track Core Web Vitals, and proactively address issues before they affect your users.
